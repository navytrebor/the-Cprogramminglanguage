# Break and Continue 

The `break` statement provides an early exit from `for` , `while`, and `do`just as from `switch`. A `break` causes the innermost enclosing loop or `switch` to be exited immediately.

The `continue`statement causes the next iteration of the emclosing `for` , `while`, and `do` loop to begin. The `continue`statement only applies to loops, not to `switch`.
